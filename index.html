U.S. power plants

The USA power plants map shows the pattern of the energy production. Energy in the United States comes mostly from fossil fuels according to the data from 2010. The data showed that 25% of the nation's energy originates from petroleum, 22% from coal, and 22% from natural gas. Nuclear energy supplied 8.4% and renewable energy supplied 8%.

Tools used
  • JSON file
  • Leafletjs
Data source: EIA's form 923 website
